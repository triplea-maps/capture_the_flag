Order goes in clockwise direction starting with Russia

Each white territory is neutral, but costs no PUs to invade

When a player loses his base, he is out for good unless he can reliberate it himself

First player starts with 12 PUs of production, and each player after this gets 3 more than the last player.

Last man, or Team standing wins (if playing a team, territory can not be shared)
